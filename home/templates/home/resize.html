Resize Instructions

Back to Add Product

Step 1

Go to img-resize.

Step 2

You will see this page

  • Under Input Image click the Choose file box.

  • Select the image from your device you wish to upload.

  • Click the Scale option and set it to width 450px height 300px.

  • Click Resize

Step 3

  • Under "Your Resized Images" click view.

  • If the result is to you liking right click and save.

  • If not happy check the Letterbox option with width 450px and height 300px and "color fill "#ffffff" and again click resize.

  • Again click view and save, (see image below)
